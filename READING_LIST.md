## Curated References (DevOps + MLOps + Data Security)

These are short, reputable standards you can cite in your deck discussion.

### MLOps / AI Risk Management
- NIST AI Risk Management Framework (AI RMF 1.0) - ([NIST AI RMF](https://www.nist.gov/itl/ai-risk-management-framework))
- NIST Privacy Framework (for privacy controls talk track) - ([NIST Privacy Framework](https://www.nist.gov/privacy-framework))

### Application / Data Security
- OWASP Top 10 (Web App Security) - ([OWASP Top 10](https://owasp.org/www-project-top-ten/))
- OWASP ML Threats - ([OWASP ML Threats](https://owasp.org/www-project-machine-learning-threats/))

### CI/CD and Containers
- Docker security guidance (best practices) - ([Docker Security](https://docs.docker.com/engine/security/))
- CIS Benchmarks (general container hardening) - ([CIS Benchmarks](https://www.cisecurity.org/cis-benchmarks))

Use these as “backup slides” if a student asks about compliance, governance, or threats.
